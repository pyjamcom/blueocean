# Asset Requirements (Visual Only)

Purpose
- Visual-only assets for 10 scene types. No text, no brands, no real people, no politics, no religion.
- Style: bold, readable shapes, clean backgrounds, high contrast, clear silhouette.

General constraints
- No text in the image.
- No logos, brands, or recognizable trademarks.
- Avoid real people or recognizable public figures.
- Keep props simple and readable at small sizes.
